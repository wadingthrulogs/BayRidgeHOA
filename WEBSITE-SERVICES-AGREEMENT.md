## RECITALS

**A.** The Client is a California nonprofit mutual benefit corporation organized to serve the homeowners of the Bay Ridge community.

**B.** The Service Provider has expertise in website design, development, and ongoing web maintenance services.

**C.** The Service Provider has designed and developed a static website for the Client, hosted at no additional cost on GitHub Pages, and the parties now wish to formalize the terms governing that work and the ongoing maintenance of the website.

The parties therefore agree as follows:

---

## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

### 1.1 Completed Work

The Service Provider has already designed and developed the Client's website (the "Website"), which includes the following pages and features:

- **Home page** — General information and welcome content for the Bay Ridge community
- **Meeting Notes page** — A section for publishing monthly board meeting notes, which authorized board members may update directly through the GitHub web interface
- **Documents page** — A hosted library of community documents including bylaws, CC&Rs, forms, and other PDFs
- **Calendar page** — A schedule of upcoming community events and board meetings
- **Contact page** — A contact form powered by Formspree, allowing community members to reach the board by email

The Website is built using Jekyll (a static site generator) and is hosted on GitHub Pages at no hosting cost to the Client.

### 1.2 Ongoing Maintenance and Support

During the Term of this Agreement, the Service Provider will provide the following ongoing services:

**(a) Content Updates.** Upon written or email request from an authorized Client representative, the Service Provider will make minor content updates to the Website, including text changes, document uploads, calendar additions, and similar edits. "Minor" updates means changes that do not require significant redesign or new feature development. Requests will be completed within **five (5) business days** of receipt, unless otherwise agreed.

**(b) Meeting Notes Support.** Board members are able to publish meeting notes directly through the GitHub web interface. If a board member requires assistance with this process, the Service Provider will provide reasonable guidance and, if needed, publish notes on the board's behalf.

**(c) Technical Maintenance.** The Service Provider will monitor the Website for technical issues, keep the Jekyll build configuration current, and address any errors or broken functionality that arise during the Term.

**(d) Uptime Monitoring.** The Service Provider will make reasonable efforts to monitor the Website's availability. Because the Website is hosted on GitHub Pages (a free third-party service), the Service Provider does not guarantee uptime but will promptly investigate and address any outages within its control.

**(e) Domain and Hosting Coordination.** If the Client holds a custom domain name, the Service Provider will assist with DNS configuration and coordination as needed to keep the Website accessible.

### 1.3 Out-of-Scope Work

The following are not included in this Agreement and would require a separate written agreement and additional fees if desired:

- Major redesigns or new feature development
- E-commerce, payment processing, or resident portal functionality
- Search engine optimization (SEO) campaigns
- Graphic design beyond minor updates consistent with the existing site design
- Migration to a different hosting platform

## 2. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 2.1 Term

This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for one (1) year, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Section ("Term").

### 2.2 Renewal

At the end of the Term, this Agreement may be renewed for additional one-year periods by mutual written agreement of both parties, executed no later than thirty (30) days before the expiration of the then-current Term.

### 2.3 Termination for Convenience

Either party may terminate this Agreement before the end of the Term by giving the other party at least **thirty (30) days' prior written notice**. Notice may be delivered by email to the address on file for each party, or by certified mail to the addresses listed above.

### 2.4 Effect of Termination

If the Client terminates this Agreement early, the Client is not entitled to a refund of any fees already paid. If the Service Provider terminates this Agreement early without cause, the Service Provider will refund a prorated portion of any prepaid fees corresponding to the remaining unused months of the Term.

Upon termination, the Service Provider will:

- Provide the Client with all website files, content, and credentials necessary to transfer or continue operating the Website
- Cooperate in good faith with any reasonable transition requests for up to fifteen (15) days following the termination date, at no additional charge
